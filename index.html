Featured Projects

Hire-Smart — AI-Driven Recruitment

Hire-Smart is an AI-driven recruitment and onboarding platform that screens resumes, ranks candidates by role fit, and automates structured interviews. It produces bias-aware shortlists, summarizes signals, and, once hired, launches onboarding checklists and tasks. Integrates with ATS/HRIS to cut time-to-hire while keeping pipelines transparent and compliant.

Design & Decode — UML Diagram Generator

Design & Decode turns plain-language prompts and code snippets into clean UML diagrams. Generate class, sequence, and activity diagrams instantly, then tweak visually. Export to SVG/PNG and Mermaid for docs. Built with NLP to map entities and flows, it speeds architecture reviews, onboarding, and reverse-engineering without heavy tooling.

Story Teller — Prompt-to-Images Narrative App

Story Teller creates illustrated stories from a single prompt. It plans plot, characters, and scenes, writes age-appropriate prose, and generates cohesive images for each page. Choose tone and art style, then export as PDF or storyboard. Safety filters and style locking keep outputs consistent for kids’ books, prototypes, and creative pitches.
